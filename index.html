About One Hyde Park

The exceptional collaboration between leading architects,
designers, artists, and hoteliers that One Hyde Park:
The Residences at Mandarin Oriental, London, delivers ultimate
perfection and a unique experience on every level.

The vision of Candy & Candy has become a reality in the creation of the most exquisite
interiors for the most exclusive address in the world. Each apartment has been designed
to complement the beautiful and carefully considered proportions of the architects`
design and every detail has been accounted for to ensure the experience of life at
One Hyde Park is unsurpassed.

Award-winning interior designers Candy & Candy have
exclusively designed all the communal areas including the spa
and recreation facilities, the grand double-height reception
space, Park Library, and Serpentine Suite as well as exclusively
designing the interior base build of each apartment.
